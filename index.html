Applying for Jobs & Getting Interviews

Advice

  • Be willing and enthusiastic to learn new things
  • Job descriptions may seem daunting, often they are asking for more than they are actually looking for
  • You will be rejected more than you will be accepted, just be patient!

Applying for jobs and getting interviews can feel incredibly daunting.

If you manage to get an interview for a tech-based job, you may be asked to complete a tech test or coding interview.

Tech tests and coding interviews can be very scary as you have to either produce work to a tight deadline or code whilst being watched.

Some things to remember:

  • You are not being judged, businesses are just looking for the perfect candidate for their job role
  • Just do your best, it's all anyone can ask for!
  • They are not designed to catch you out, a lot aren't even designed to be completed
  • How you approach and handle the task is just as important as how you code the task
  • Try not to get flustered - a calm and organised person who doesn't get lots of coding done will appear better than someone who gets flustered but codes lots. Most likely the flustered person will make coding mistakes!
  • If you are being watched whilst you code, voice what decisions you are making and why you are making them
