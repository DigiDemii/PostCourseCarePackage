Self-learning is a vital skill to have if you want to become a software developer.

Quite often you will find yourself working on something alone with only yourself to fix whatever problem you may be having.

From YouTube videos to online courses, there's so many ways you can continue your learning and build up your portfolio!

There are many ways you can continue your studies:

  • Take another CodeNation course, we have many to offer!
  • Join one of the many free websites listed above to develop your skills
  • Watch YouTube videos and use what you learn to build your GitHub portfolio
  • Take one of many online courses to not only learn but earn yourself a certificate!
